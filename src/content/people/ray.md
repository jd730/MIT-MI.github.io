Ray Song is a researcher focusing on Multimodal Sensing, Digital Fabrication and Human-Robot Interaction (HRI). Ray is a graduate student at MIT Media Lab [Multisensory Intelligence](https://www.media.mit.edu/groups/multisensory-intelligence/overview/) research group, advised by [Prof. Paul Liang](https://pliang279.github.io/) (Media Lab) and [Prof. Wojciech Matusik](https://cdfg.csail.mit.edu/wojciech) (EECS). 

Ray received his B.S. in Computer Science at the University of Washington (UW), working with [Prof. Siddhartha Srinivasa](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~siddh/), [Prof. Maya Cakmak](https://www.mayacakmak.io/), [Prof. Yiyue Luo](https://yyueluo.com/).
