Media Lab Professor [Paul Liang](/people/paul) participated in Honeywell’s AIML Summit, where he presented on vibration analytics, the theme of this year’s Hackathon. His session explored how machine learning can be applied to detect anomalies and predict mechanical failures, reinforcing the role of AI in industrial reliability and predictive maintenance.
